The Game
The main objective of the game is to achieve the most points in the time you have managed to accumulate.
You have to have the dexterity, agility and reflexes necessary to shoot the coins and avoid the bombs!
On the playing field there will be bombs that subtract points, blue coins rarer than the common ones that give extra points, a clock that gives the player more time and an additional special objective of Highest rarity, which provides the most points.
The bomb is -10 points.
The normal Coin is just 1 point.
Extras
The game has aScoreboard which records player scores.
At the end of the game you will have the option to enter your name to include yourself in it.

In addition to the points you make, you can earn achievements, which are visible in the of Achievements.
